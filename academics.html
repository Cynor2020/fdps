Academic Excellence

Our Academic Philosophy

At Farmer's Den Public School, we are committed to academic excellence through a well-balanced curriculum. We focus on both intellectual growth and the development of strong character. Our programs are designed to foster creativity, critical thinking, and a passion for lifelong learning.

Curriculum Structure

Early Years (Nursery & Kindergarten)

Focus on play-based learning, foundational literacy and numeracy skills, and social-emotional development in a nurturing environment.

Primary School (Grades 1–5)

Core subjects include English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies, supported by creative arts, physical education, and digital literacy.

Middle & Senior School (Grades 6–10)

Students are challenged with advanced coursework, critical thinking projects, and opportunities for leadership. We prepare students for board examinations and beyond.

Beyond the Classroom

In addition to academics, our students participate in a wide range of co-curricular activities such as sports, music, drama, robotics, coding clubs, and debate. These activities help students discover and develop their unique talents.
