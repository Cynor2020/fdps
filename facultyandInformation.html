About Our School

Our school was founded in 2016 by the visionary educator Mr. Panditrao Yadavrao Chandgude with a mission to transform the educational landscape. Over the years, we've grown from a small learning center to a thriving academic community known for excellence in teaching and values-based education. Our core belief is in nurturing every child's potential and fostering innovation through collaborative learning.

With a highly experienced staff and modern infrastructure, we provide a balanced mix of academic, sports, and creative opportunities to help students become responsible global citizens. Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields across India and the world.
